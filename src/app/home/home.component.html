Personal insurance business caters to the needs of the individuals and families and protects the individuals from losses they couldn’t afford to cover on their own. Commercial insurance is insurance that protects businesses. It protects businesses against loss or damage through unforeseen circumstances like Property damage, Business Interruption, theft, burglary, liability, road accidents to fleet of vehicles, workmen’s injuries, marine and aviation accidents etc. Commercial insurance comes in many forms, and what you’ll need will depend upon your business
